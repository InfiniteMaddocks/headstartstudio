About HeadStart Studio

HeadStart Studio is built on a simple idea: adult film work should be conducted with the same level of professionalism and respect as any other production. That means clear communication, written agreements, ID verification, defined boundaries, and a safe set.

We are based in New York and operate on a small, controlled set environment — no party chaos, no surprises, and no pressure to do anything outside of the fully agreed plan.

What We Stand For

  • Respect for all performers
  • Clearly defined roles and expectations
  • Transparent compensation and agreements
  • Legal compliance and documentation
  • Clean, safe, private filming environment

Current Casting Information

Our Process

1

Initial Contact

You send us an email with your basic info and a recent photo. We review your information and reply if there may be a fit for upcoming projects.

2

Screening & Questions

We schedule a call or message exchange to discuss boundaries, limits, expectations, and answer any questions you have before moving forward.

3

ID Verification & Paperwork

Before any filming, we verify your age and identity with a valid government ID, and review and sign all required documents, including consent and model release forms.

4

Production Day

On shoot day, we walk through the plan again, confirm your boundaries, and maintain a professional, respectful environment at all times. You may decline or change your comfort level at any point before filming.

5

Payment & Follow-Up

Compensation is handled according to the agreement discussed before filming. We make sure you leave with clarity about how the content may be used and how to reach us with any follow-up questions.

18+ Age & Legal Compliance

Age Verification

All performers must provide valid, government-issued photo identification proving they are at least 18 years old on the day of filming. We keep secure records as required by U.S. law.

We do not work with anyone under 18 under any circumstances, and we do not accept falsified documents.

Record-Keeping (2257)

HeadStart Studio maintains documentation in accordance with applicable U.S. laws, including 18 U.S.C. 2257 record-keeping requirements, where applicable.

A full 2257 compliance statement and records custodian information will be provided to any qualified party upon proper request.
